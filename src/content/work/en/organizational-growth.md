When I started, the concept of "behavioral science consulting" barely existed in China's enterprise landscape. The challenge wasn't just doing the work — it was building an entire industry category from scratch.

I approached it the way I approach every problem: systematically. I built the methodology, created the delivery framework, trained the team, and established the market positioning. Each step was deliberate, each process was documented, and each success became a template for the next.

The result: 243% year-over-year growth, transforming from a solo practitioner into a complete consulting system. More importantly, we proved that behavioral science isn't just an academic discipline — it's a powerful business tool when applied with rigor and creativity.
