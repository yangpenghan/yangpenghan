A luxury automotive brand needed more than ad-hoc UX testing — they needed a systematic, repeatable evaluation framework that could scale across product lines and model years.

I designed the methodology from the ground up: integrating eye tracking, behavioral coding, and cognitive load analysis into a unified evaluation system. The framework covered the entire HMI interaction lifecycle — from initial visual hierarchy assessment through in-drive cognitive load measurement.

What started as a single project evolved into an annual framework partnership at the million-level. The brand now uses our evaluation system as a standard part of their product development pipeline, demonstrating that behavioral science can deliver measurable ROI in the automotive industry.
